roundedcorner - Enable rounded corner (won't work when 'circular' is enabled) (disabled by default).
rotated - Lift the images with flat bottom (disabled by default).
circular - Crop the images in circular (enabled by default).
crop - Crop the images, instead of enlarge them with alpha (disabled by default).
trim - Trim to smallest size by removing transparent pixels (enabled by default).
shadow - Add shadow to image (not implemented yet) (enabled by default).
higher - Lift the image high (won't work when 'rotated' is enabled) (disabled by default).
keeparea - Leave the area unchanged (center by default).
reset - Delete your file.
help - Show this helpful message.
settings - show current settings.
